Awards page

Honoring Contributions with Swami Brahmanand Award

Established on the 125th birth anniversary of Swami Brahmanand ji, the award commemorates his remarkable contributions to society and encourages meaningful service in Education and Goseva.

Cash Prize

A recognition amount for the awardee.

Commemorative Statue

A symbolic statue of Swami Brahmanand ji.

Bronze Medal

A formal medal presented at the ceremony.

Certificate & Shawl

Recognition in print and ceremonial honor.

Award Components

What the award includes

  • Cash Prize of 10,000 INR
  • Commemorative statue of Swami Brahmanand Ji
  • Prestigious bronze medal
  • Formal certificate of recognition
  • Ceremonial shawl as a token of honor
Eligibility

Who can be nominated?

  • Individuals from India or abroad.
  • People with notable contributions to education and/or Goseva.
  • Nominees reviewed by the award committee through its selection process.
Award history

Why the award matters.

The award was created to honor service-minded contributions that reflect the values associated with Swami Brahmanand ji and to encourage individuals and organizations whose work exemplifies excellence and innovation.

Nomination

Do you know someone who has made a remarkable impact in Education?

Nominate them for the SB Awards! We are looking for individuals and organizations whose work exemplifies excellence and innovation.

Nomination Criteria

  • Significant contributions to Education
  • Demonstrated innovation and leadership
  • Positive impact on the community
  • Significant contributions to Goseva

Nomination Process

  • Complete the nomination form Download Form
  • Provide detailed information about the nominee's achievements and impact.
  • Submit your nomination from 11/07/2026 till 15/08/2026
  • Awardee Announcement: 25/08/2026
  • Award Ceremony Date: 13/09/2026

Note

  1. The proposer should send their proposal on A4-sized paper describing their activities (for which the applicant is awarded) in favor of the applicant for the award, along with this application.
  2. Send HD pictures showing two close-up profiles of the applicant and three activities.
  3. The proposer should fill out this application form and mail it to thesbaward@gmail.com with all the attachments.
