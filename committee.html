Committee

Meet the people behind the award.

Award Committee is a distinguished group of leaders, educators, and social advocates dedicated to upholding Swami ji’s legacy. Together, they volunteer their expertise to carefully identify and celebrate the unsung heroes making extraordinary, grassroots contributions to education and go seva (cow welfare) across the nation.
