We promote awareness and encourage contributions from the elite society in the fields of education and cow protection. Our mission is to highlight exemplary work and inspire meaningful action in these crucial areas.

This award aims to honor and encourage individuals and organizations committed to strengthening the traditions of education and cow service. This initiative is the result of the dedicated efforts of all our volunteers and committee members, whose shared vision and tireless work made this idea a reality. It reflects our collective resolve to continue Swamiji‚Äôs legacy and to promote service-oriented action for the greater good of society.
