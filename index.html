Concept
How it started

In June 2019, to celebrate Swami Brahmanand Ji's 125th birth anniversary with joy and enthusiasm, an experiment was started by Guinness World Record holder Shailendra Patel to make a statue of Swami Ji from grains, which could have failed.

Keeping this in mind, Narendra Singh Lodhi, teacher, resident of village- Chandarsi, district- Jalaun, who is serving as a teacher in district Chitrakoot, thought about the Swami Brahmanand Award and put forward his idea in the group of Lakshya Parivar Rath, in which some members expressed disagreement and said that let the statue work be completed now, after that we will think about it next year, but Narendra Singh kept on conceptualizing the award and kept on preparing to give this honor to the people doing special work in various fields in Bundelkhand region. Thought of giving awards in education, art, writing, sports, agriculture, and environment every year. This idea was shared with drug dealer Manohar Singh Rajput of Pawai, Sarila, Hamirpur, and Haricharan Fauji Gaurav Senani, Tola Rawat, Rath, and asked for cooperation in starting the award. In September 2019, writer-journalist Amit Rajput discussed the Swami Brahmanand Award with teacher Narendra Singh. Narendra Singh stated that he has been preparing the outline for the award for the last four months, and it is scheduled to begin on the 125th birth anniversary of Swami Ji. He told Amit Rajput the entire outline. This Narendra Lodhi had sown the seeds of contemplation about Swami Brahmanand within journalist/writer Amit Rajput. Amit Rajput felt the need for some changes in it and gave the idea of giving it in only two areas, education and cow service, and it was considered to be given at the national/international level instead of only Bundelkhand. Both of them agreed on this and soon started the process of establishing the Swami Brahmanand Award. After the outline was finalized, the idea of the award was put forward respectively in front of famous dentist of Rath city Dr. Brajesh Rajput Atraulia, then national convener of Lakshya Amarpal Singh Lodhi IRS, Dr. Mukesh Rajput (Environmental Scientist), businessman by profession Devendra Mahan, Sikrodha Hamirpur, Hari Mohan Singh Rajput, teacher, resident - Puraini, Sarila, Hamirpur Pushpendra Singh Rajput teacher Atgaon Rath, administrative officer Gulab Singh, Gohand, Dr. Drigpal Singh, Bhainsa Sarila, Dr. Ranvijay Singh Veterinary Officer, Rath and Dr. Bhanu Pratap Singh Veterinary Officer, resident - Bangara Rath, which almost everyone accepted unanimously with great enthusiasm and as a result of deep discussion, 'Swami Brahmanand Award Committee' was formed. Manohar Singh was elected unanimously as President, Narendra Singh Lodhi as Vice President, Harimohan Singh as Secretary, Devendra Mahan as Joint Secretary, Pushpendra Singh as Treasurer, and Dr. Brajesh Rajput as Auditor. Dr. Bhanu Pratap Singh, Dr. Mukesh Rajput, Dr. Ranvijay Singh, and Dr. Drigpal Singh were nominated as members of the Executive Committee. It was decided unanimously that the 'Swami Brahmanand Award' would be given every year for the special contributions of Swami Brahmanand Ji in education and cow service.
