We promote awareness and encourage contributions from the elite society in the fields of education and cow protection. Our mission is to highlight exemplary work and inspire meaningful action in these crucial areas.

About

Tyagmurti Swami Brahmanand

Swami Brahmanand ji was born on 04 December 1894 AD in village Barhara of Hamirpur district in Bundelkhand region of Uttar Pradesh. Swami Brahmanand's father was Matadin Lodhi, who belonged to the prosperous farming family of the region. His mother's name was Yashodabai who was a housewife of religious nature. Swami Brahmanand's childhood name was Shivdayal. Swami Brahmanand ji's warning education took place in the village itself.

Award History
Honoring a Legacy

Established on the 125th birth anniversary of Swami Brahmanand Ji, this award commemorates his remarkable contributions to society. It was initiated to encourage and reward those who uphold his legacy in education and Goseva, bringing transformative changes to their communities.

Application Criteria
Eligibility
  • Open to both Indian and non-Indian individuals.
  • Nominees must demonstrate significant contributions in education and/or cow protection.
  • Awardees are selected through a rigorous evaluation process conducted by the Award Committee.
